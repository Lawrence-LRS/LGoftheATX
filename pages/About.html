About me

Hello world - Ha, my name is Lawrence Goedrich, I graduated as a Mechanical engineer and untill recently I was a rocket design engineer. I worked on critical engine components, designing and running structual and thermal components. I have been convinced to start learning about programming, with little experience and a desire to make games I started fiddling on the unity game engine. During a span of two weeks I was hooked on making games in C# completing the five tutorial games and my own peace of music visualization software. What I really wanted to do was learn how to make virtual reality games but during the process of making the visualization peace I realized I was missing some fundamental knowledge and decided to train myself before jumping head first.

It was at this point that my computer programmer friends had suggested that I start taking lessons using the Codeacademy as a templete for future developement. Armed with all the freetime I could imagine, I have set off to learn as much as I can, tackling the fullstack plan to learn; HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery, AngularJS, Command, Ruby, GIT and much more. At the end of this experience I hope to have enough knowledge and prior expereince with programming to start working as a CS-Intern helping start-ups develop their ideas while going back to making games and my own dreams.

The future is a wonderful mystery.
