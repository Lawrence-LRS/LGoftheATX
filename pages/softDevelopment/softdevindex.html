3D music visualizer

I created a 3D music visualizer using the unity game engine and C# scripting. This music visualizer takes an audio input and creates either a full circle, half circle or straight wall of rectangles or cylinders. These shapes can then be directed to move up or out to create the desired effects. I have various options listed on my Fiverr for add-ons, including sprite backgrounds such as sunset or stars, floating UI to provide artist names, song titles or logo/icons, and even some particle effects. The particle effects are created with the unity provided engine to create fireworks from the center of the circle/half circle or have snow fall over the camera lense.
