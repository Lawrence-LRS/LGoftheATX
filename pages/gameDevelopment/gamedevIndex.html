SpaceCowboy Gamebook

This is a text based choose your own adventure game. The main object of the game is to explore the spaceship and await Johns return. You are given the choices to explore for treasures, go check out various noises, or sit tight and wait for John. This game was developed completely in JS-vanilla making the most difficult part getting asynchronous commands without ajax. JS by nature is multi-threaded, but a choose your own adventure, with text input is a single-threaded application. This was accomplished with multiple callbacks and nested listening events that would wait for user input before continuing. The second challenge while trivial was getting the text to delay when posting to the the dialoge box wich is all ran on second delays from initial starts, or else it would show up out of order or pages at a time making it impossible to play.
